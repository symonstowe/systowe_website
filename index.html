About

I am a professional data scientist who specializes in working with a wide range of signals to uncover underlying trends in data. In my coursework and professional experience I have leveraged machine learning to inform high-risk decisions.

I love searching for overlooked markers and features that reveal meaningful insights. I have experience working with a wide range of teams across different management styles, an extensive teaching background and a passion for helping others.
